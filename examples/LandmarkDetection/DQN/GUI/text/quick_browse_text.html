Browse Mode

For more information: Ctrl+H


1. Load data: choose defaults, or browse custom data.
2. Enable HITL: toggle checkbox to enable storing of interaction data.
3. Navigation: press one of the six Human Actions or keyboard shortcuts (w = +y, s = -y, a = -x, d = +x, z = +z, x = -z) to navigate through the enviroment.
4. Next image: press Next Image to move to the next loaded image.
5. Save data: to save interaction data, toggle off Enable HITL, or close the GUI.
